Σχετικά με εμένα

It is well known that Martial Arts equips people with the tools to defend themselves from a physical threat or assault but at Sanctuary Martial Arts our concept is to give you not only the tools to defend yourself physically, but also mentally and emotionally. Low self esteem, lack of confidence, anxiety and stress are just a few of the challenges that can threaten our wellbeing. With meditation to centre and calm the body and mind, Qi Gong and Tai chi to focus you, physical exercise to build up the serotonin levels and simply a safe place to reset...what we offer you is SANCTUARY.
